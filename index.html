AI Processing

When you record a voice note, the audio is sent to Anthropic's Claude AI API for analysis. The AI extracts trade-related information (counterparty names, ISIN codes, amounts, settlement details) and returns structured data to auto-fill your form.

⚡

Audio recordings are transmitted to Anthropic's servers solely for real-time processing. They are not stored, retained, or used for AI training purposes. The audio is processed and immediately discarded by Anthropic's systems.

For more information about Anthropic's data handling practices, please refer to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

💾

Data Storage & Security

All trade data you enter or that is extracted by AI remains on your device's local storage. We do not maintain servers or databases that store your trade information. When you export data to Excel, the file is generated locally on your device.

We implement reasonable security measures appropriate for a client-side application, including secure API communication over HTTPS for AI processing requests.

Your Rights

Since your data stays on your device, you have full control at all times.

✓ Delete all data by uninstalling the app
✓ Clear trade data at any time within the app
✓ Revoke microphone permission in iOS Settings
✓ Use the app in manual mode without voice
✓ Export your data in Excel format anytime
✓ No account or registration required

Children's Privacy

PostTradeRFI is a professional financial tool not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has used this app, the data exists only on that device and can be removed by uninstalling the app.
